DEADMAG · CHEAT CODES
=====================

Solo modes only. Open the cheat panel by clicking the version label
in the corner, type a code, and hit ENTER (or APPLY).

CODES
-----

money            — Set your cash to $999,999.
max money        — Alias for "money".

god              — Toggle invincibility (you take no damage).
invincible       — Alias for "god".
invincibility    — Alias for "god".

one shot         — Toggle one-shot kills (your bullets, rockets, and
                   melee instantly destroy any zombie, including
                   armored brutes).
oneshot          — Alias.
1shot            — Alias.
instakill        — Alias.

all normal       — Stop forcing a zombie type; spawns return to the
                   normal wave-based mix.
normal horde     — Alias for "all normal".

all sprinter     — Every zombie that spawns is a SPRINTER (fast,
                   fragile, comes in packs).
all sprinters    — Alias.
sprinter horde   — Alias.

all brute        — Every zombie that spawns is a BRUTE (slow, armored
                   tank with three plates).
all brutes       — Alias.
brute horde      — Alias.

all volt-fuse    — Every zombie that spawns is a VOLT-FUSE (exploder;
                   detonates on proximity or after ~6s).
all voltfuse     — Alias.
all exploder     — Alias.
all exploders    — Alias.
volt horde       — Alias.

NOTES
-----
- "all <kind>" cheats override the round-based spawn weighting, so
  you can fight a single type from round 1.
- Re-typing the same toggle cheat (god) flips it off.
- Cheats are blocked in multiplayer — the server is authoritative.
